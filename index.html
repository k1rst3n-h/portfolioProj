About me

Hi, I’m Kirsten. I’m a passionate developer who finds joy in combining logic and creativity through code. I’m especially driven by the challenge of building thoughtful, well-designed solutions — and continuously learning along the way. Outside of coding, I’m deeply interested in health, fitness, and nutrition. While these fields may seem unrelated, I believe they complement each other well. A disciplined body supports a sharp and focused mind — something that benefits my development work greatly. I approach both coding and fitness with consistency, structure, and a growth mindset. Whether it’s writing clean code or hitting a new goal at the gym, I’m always aiming to improve and evolve.
